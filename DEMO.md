https://montgomery-civic-intelligence.vercel.app/

This demo takes about **60–90 seconds** and shows how Montgomery Civic Intelligence transforms public permit data into clear civic insights.

---

## Step 1 — Explore Housing Development

Open the live application and select:

Explore Data → Housing & Development → Housing activity (last 60 days)

The system will generate an AI summary explaining:

• how many residential permits were issued  
• where housing activity is concentrated  
• the most common permit types

The response includes transparency indicators such as:

- data freshness  
- date window reliability  
- geographic coverage

---

## Step 2 — Review Civic Intelligence Overview

Click **Overview** at the top of the page.

This dashboard summarizes recent construction activity using key metrics:

• residential permits  
• commercial permits  
• new construction vs repairs  
• top development corridor

These metrics are generated directly from Montgomery's open permit dataset.

---

## Step 3 — View Source Verification

Scroll to the **External verification sources** section below the AI response.

These links come from official municipal sources such as:

- City of Montgomery pages
- meeting agendas
- open data resources

This allows users to verify projects and civic activity through official public sources.

---

## Step 4 — Ask a Custom Question

Use **Ask a custom question** and try:

Example:

What commercial construction activity has been happening recently in Montgomery?

The AI will analyze recent permit data and produce a structured civic summary.

---

## What This Demonstrates

Montgomery Civic Intelligence shows how AI can transform municipal open data into understandable civic insights.

The platform helps residents quickly understand:

- housing development patterns  
- commercial construction activity  
- where development is happening  
- how permit activity is changing over time
